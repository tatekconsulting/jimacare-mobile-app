## Overview
This is the **Provider/Carer App** - for care providers who offer services.

## Features
- Browse available jobs
- Manage availability status
- Accept/decline bookings
- Track location during jobs
- View earnings & analytics
- Manage profile & documents
- Video calls with clients
- View job applications

## App Configuration
- Package: `com.jimacare.provider`
- App Name: "JimaCare Provider"
- Target Users: Care providers/carers
